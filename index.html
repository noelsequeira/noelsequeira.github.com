Creative Riot Software

Startup,  2009 - Present

Creative Riot was founded in 2009, fueled by the idea of building elegant, easy to deploy, usable applications to help IT departments effectively manage thousands of users. We're an incubatee at the Center for Innovation, Incubation and Entrepreneurship at IIM Ahmedabad. We've worked with some of the largest companies across India including Capgemini, Cognizant, DHL, the Aditya Birla Group, Persistent Systems, Mahindra & Mahindra, Honeywell, Emerson, HCC, Bajaj Allianz, JM Financial, Mastek and several more; helping them better understand their IT energy footprints.
 
Enpower

Product,  2008 - Present

Enpower is a comprehensive enterprise IT power management and monitoring solution for PCs, Power over Ethernet devices and Servers; used across thousands of endpoints daily by customers including Persistent Systems, DHL and Corelogic.
 
 
Time Trove

Product,  2010 - Present

Time Trove is fully automated software for time tracking and productivity analysis, for power users and teams who want granular insights into how they spend their time.
CRMinbox

Experiment,  2011 - Present

CRMinbox is a Chrome extension for GMail that serves as a bridge between your CRM and your email client, and lets you track accounts, deals and people from within the inbox. We built it to eliminate the overhead of context switching between the CRM and email information silos.
 
 
Mumbai Blood

Project,  2007 - 2008

Mumbai Blood Donors is a [now defunct] local online voluntary blood donor directory. It was a flawed attempt to help people in urgent need of blood find a voluntary donor. This is a subject that's particularly close to heart, and lately I've come to believe that a sustainable solution to what's largely a set of logistical challenges lies in motivating donors to visit blood banks regularly to replenish dwindling supplies in addition to taking drives to the donors (the latter is what happens now).
