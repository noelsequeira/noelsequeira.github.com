Firmzen

Startup,  2012 - Present

Enpower (Creative Riot Software)

Startup,  2009 - 2012

Creative Riot was founded in 2009, fueled by the idea of building elegant, easy to deploy, usable applications to help IT departments effectively manage thousands of users. Our flagship product, Enpower, is a comprehensive enterprise IT power management and monitoring solution for PCs, Power over Ethernet devices and Servers; used across thousands of endpoints daily by customers including Persistent Systems, DHL and Corelogic.

We are an incubatee at the CIIE, at IIM Ahmedabad. We've worked with some of the largest companies across India including Capgemini, Cognizant, DHL, the Aditya Birla Group, Persistent Systems, Mahindra & Mahindra, Honeywell, Emerson, HCC, Bajaj Allianz, JM Financial, Mastek and several more; helping them better understand their IT energy footprints.
